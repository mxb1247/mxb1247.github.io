Stonehenge

As I saw the stones from the distance I started getting chills. The place was magical and the view was breath taking. When I finally arrived to the top of the mountain I could appreciate how the stones were perfectly placed in a circle which makes you wonder about the mystery of the stones and its creation. The feeling of grandiosity and excitement that this sight gives you is incredible. Stonehenge is a must due when you are in London, it is defiantly a once in a lifetime opportunity.
